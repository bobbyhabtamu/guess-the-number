Number Guessing Game

Welcome to the number guessing game! The computer will pick a random number between 1 and 10. Your goal is to guess the number in as few attempts as possible.

To play:

1.Enter your guess in the text box. Your guess must be a number between 1 and 10.

2.Click the "Guess" button to submit your guess.

3.The computer will tell you if your guess is too high, too low, or correct!

4.If your guess is incorrect, try again until you guess the number.

5.Once you guess correctly, the game will display how many guesses it took you and a congratulatory message.

6.Refresh the page to play again and see if you can beat your previous number of guesses!

7.Good luck! See how quickly you can guess the computer's random number.
